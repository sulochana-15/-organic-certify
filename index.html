💡 Cost Saving Tips

  • 🏛️ Apply for government subsidies — up to 50% subsidy available for small farmers
  • 👥 Join a PGS group for much lower certification costs
  • 📝 Prepare all documents yourself to avoid consultant fees
  • 🤝 Group certification with neighboring farms reduces per-farm cost
  • 📅 Apply during off-peak season to avoid rush surcharges
